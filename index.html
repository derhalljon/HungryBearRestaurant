We are one of the best barbecue restaurants in the country. Back in 1839 my great grandfather, seeing huge potential, built his first log cabin restaurnt on the edge of Edson, Alberta, Canada, to serve his barbecue to the local saw mills and towns. Little did he know that his expertise in barbecue would make people start flocking to his little cabin.
